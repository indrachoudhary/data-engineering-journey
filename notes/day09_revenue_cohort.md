# Day 09 - Revenue Cohort Analysis

## What is Revenue Cohort?

Instead of tracking user activity,
we track revenue generated by each cohort over time.

Example:
Users who signed up in Jan 2024:
- Month 0 revenue
- Month 1 revenue
- Month 2 revenue

This helps measure:
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Revenue growth quality
- Customer value over time

--------------------------------------------------

## Tables Assumed

users(user_id, signup_date)
orders(user_id, order_date, amount)

--------------------------------------------------

## Step 1 - Find Cohort Month

DATE_TRUNC('month', signup_date)

--------------------------------------------------

## Step 2 - Find Revenue Month

DATE_TRUNC('month', order_date)

--------------------------------------------------

## Step 3 - Calculate Month Difference

DATE_PART('month',
    AGE(revenue_month, cohort_month)
)

--------------------------------------------------

## Step 4 - Aggregate Revenue

SUM(amount)

--------------------------------------------------

## Business Meaning

Month 0 → revenue in signup month  
Month 1 → revenue in next month  
Month 2 → second month  

Used to calculate LTV curves.

--------------------------------------------------

## Key Learning

- Cohort logic reused
- Replace COUNT with SUM(amount)
- Used for financial analytics
